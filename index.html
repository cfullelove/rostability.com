About:

Rostability is a cloud-based application that enables small business owners to manage staff availability, rosters and timesheets. The application has been designed to be simple to use by business owners, managers and their staff.

Features:

  • Simple web-based interface
  • Works great on mobile
  • Capture and manage staff availability
  • Create and manage store rosters
  • Record actual hours in timesheets
  • Email & SMS notifications
  • Multiple stores supported
  • Customisable Reporting
  • iCal Export
