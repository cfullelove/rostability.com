About

Rostability is a cloud-based application that enables small business owners to manage staff availability, rosters and timesheets. The application has been designed to be simple to use by business owners, managers and their staff.

Availability

Simply capture your staff availability so you always know who can work when. Easily allow staff to submit their availability from the web or on their phone.

Roster

Quickly and easily roster available staff for each shift and store. Once you're done, publish the roster via the web, email and SMS. Last minute changes are handled too by easily presenting staff and managers with possible fill-ins.

Timesheets

Capture the hours worked by your staff in timesheets. Featuring MYOB and Excel exporting in addition to included reporting.
