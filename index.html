About

Rostability is a cloud-based application that enables small business owners to manage staff availability, rosters and timesheets. It’s light-weight and cloud hosted (so you don’t have to install anything!).

Rostability frees you from the hassle of spreadsheets and paper rosters and lets you focus on running your business

Availability

Simply capture your staff availability so you always know who can work when. Allow staff to easily submit their availability from the web or on their phone.

Roster

Quickly and easily roster available staff for each shift and store. Once you're done, publish the roster via the web, email and SMS at the click of a button. Last minute changes are handled too by presenting staff and managers with possible fill-ins.

Timesheets

Capture the hours worked by your staff in timesheets. Featuring MYOB and Excel exporting in addition to included reporting to analyse staff labour costs
